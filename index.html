The CXF Methodology

CarbonXFuture has developed its own verified carbon credit standard — CXF — specifically designed for waste oil collection and recycling infrastructure. Applicants earn digital CXF credits tradeable directly on our platform.

STEP 01

Apply & document

Waste oil collectors and recyclers submit operational data — volumes collected, processing method, emission factors — through our standardized CXF application protocol.

STEP 02

Third-party verification

Independent auditors validate emission reductions against the CXF baseline methodology. MRV (Monitoring, Reporting & Verification) aligned with international carbon standards.

STEP 03

Receive digital CXF credits

Upon verification, applicants receive CXF digital credits — recorded in our registry, instantly tradeable on the CarbonXFuture marketplace or retirable against corporate targets.

CXF — the digital carbon credit for circular oil economy

Each CXF credit represents 1 tonne of CO₂ equivalent avoided through verified waste oil collection and processing. Credits are issued as digital tokens on our platform registry, with full traceability from source facility to retirement.
